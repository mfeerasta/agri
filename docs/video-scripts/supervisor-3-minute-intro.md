# Supervisor intro video · 3 minutes

Target: 2 supervisors at Rupafab Agri, Raiwind.
Length: 3 minutes.
Format: 16:9 horizontal for tablet viewing.

## Production notes

- Shoot on the same Android tablet supervisors will carry.
- One day in the life structure, morning to evening.
- Voice talent: Urdu first, English subtitles. Calm and authoritative tone, not folksy.
- Pace: brisk but not rushed. Three real screen demos, not slideware.

## Shot list

### Scene 1 · Cold open and role (0:00 to 0:20)

Visual: Wide shot of fields at dawn. Supervisor walking out of the farm office with tablet in hand.

VO (UR): "سپروائزر۔ آپ کا کام ورکروں اور فارم منیجر کے درمیان پل۔"
VO (Roman UR): "Supervisor. Aap ka kaam workers aur farm manager ke darmiyan pul."

On-screen English: "Supervisor: the bridge between workers and the farm manager."

### Scene 2 · Morning routine, ops app (0:20 to 0:55)

Visual: Tablet screen recording. Supervisor opens ops.agri.feerasta.ai. Lands on dashboard. Pans across attendance card, task list, diesel reading.

VO (UR): "صبح۔ آپس ایپ کھولیں۔ پہلے حاضری، پھر آج کے کام، پھر ڈیزل کی ابتدائی ریڈنگ۔"

VO English (subtitle): "Morning: open the ops app. Attendance first, then today's tasks, then diesel opening reading."

Cuts to the supervisor walking to the diesel tank, dipping a measuring rod, then typing the reading on the tablet.

### Scene 3 · Assigning tasks (0:55 to 1:25)

Visual: Screen recording of `/assign` flow. Pick field F3. Pick activity "irrigation". Add three workers. Set expected hours to 6. Add Urdu note. Submit.

VO (UR): "کام بانٹیں۔ فیلڈ چنیں، سرگرمی چنیں، ورکر چنیں، گھنٹے لکھیں، نوٹ لکھیں، بھیج دیں۔ ورکر کے فون پر فوراً اطلاع پہنچے گی۔"

VO English (subtitle): "Assign work. Field, activity, workers, hours, note, send. Workers get a push instantly."

### Scene 4 · Midday inspection (1:25 to 1:55)

Visual: Supervisor walking through wheat. Phone (tablet) up, opens `/inspect/[cropPlanId]`. Takes three photos: wide, mid, close-up. Voice-records observation. Flags a mild pest.

VO (UR): "دوپہر۔ معائنہ۔ تین تصویریں۔ آواز سے مشاہدات۔ کیڑا نظر آئے، نشان لگاؤ۔ فارم منیجر کو فوری اطلاع جاتی ہے۔"

VO English (subtitle): "Midday inspection. Three photos. Voice observations. Flag any pest, farm manager gets an instant alert."

### Scene 5 · Approvals and triage (1:55 to 2:25)

Visual: Tablet shows the supervisor's approvals queue. Three items: a 3,000 rupee repair (within authority), a 12,000 rupee repair (escalates to farm manager), a 60,000 rupee asset purchase (escalates to MF).

VO (UR): "اجازت کی حدود۔ پانچ ہزار تک آپ خود۔ پچیس ہزار تک فارم منیجر۔ زیادہ ہو تو ایم ایف۔ شک ہو، اوپر بھیجیں۔ نقصان نہیں۔"

VO English (subtitle): "Approval ceilings. Up to 5k you. Up to 25k farm manager. Above, MF. When in doubt, escalate."

### Scene 6 · Evening reconciliation (2:25 to 2:50)

Visual: Sunset. Supervisor at the diesel tank with a second person witnessing. Dips the rod, reads, types on tablet. Screen shows variance calculation: opening minus issued minus closing equals expected. Green tick.

VO (UR): "شام۔ ڈیزل کا ملان۔ گواہ کے ساتھ۔ پانچ فیصد سے زیادہ فرق ہو تو فارم منیجر کو فوراً بتائیں۔"

VO English (subtitle): "Evening diesel reconciliation. With a witness. Variance above 5 percent, call the farm manager."

### Scene 7 · Daily digest (2:50 to 3:00)

Visual: Tablet screen, the auto-generated daily digest. Supervisor reviews, edits one line, taps "send to MF".

VO (UR): "روزانہ خلاصہ MF کو۔ ساڑھے سات بجے۔ تصدیق کرکے بھیجیں۔"

VO English (subtitle): "Daily digest to MF at 19:30. Review, send."

End card: "ops.agri.feerasta.ai"
