Target: MF.
Length: 60 seconds.
Format: 9:16 vertical, for viewing on iPhone where MF will actually approve.
Tone: dry, efficient, no ceremony.

## Production notes

VO: "Cash on hand. Three quotes side by side. The reason the farm manager picked this one. The asset history is one tap away."

VO: "Approve. GPS, IP, time captured. Audit done. The work order is dispatched automatically."

### Scene 5 · Back to your day (0:55 to 1:00)

VO: "Forty seconds, start to finish. That is the point."
