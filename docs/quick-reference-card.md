## Side B · Roman Urdu and English

### Five big actions

✅ Hazri (Attendance)
- Subah, sabz button, GPS green tick

📋 Kaam (Tasks)
- Aaj ka kaam dekho, photo lagao, complete par tick

⛽ Diesel
- Har baar, receipt ki photo lazmi (mandatory)

🛠 Marammat (Repair)
- Toota, kharab: photo, severity, send

🎤 Awaaz (Voice)
- Likhna mushkil, mic dabao, bolo

### Voice phrases that work

- "F3 paani laga 6 ghante"  (F3 watered, 6 hours)
- "Tractor T1 mein 20 litre diesel"  (20 L diesel in T1)
- "F7 mein spray ho gayi"  (Spraying done in F7)
- "Tubewell number 2 band hai"  (Tubewell 2 is down)
- "F12 mein hazri laga, baad mein nikla"  (Marked in at F12, left later)

### Receipt rules

- Always photo, always before pocketing the receipt.
- Pump receipt in English is fine. Photo is what matters.
- If receipt is missing, log it anyway and note "no receipt" in the voice field. Supervisor will follow up.
